The Stones We Trust
DIAMONDS

Our diamonds are selected with care, not just by certification, but for the light they hold and the feeling they evoke. Every stone is sourced from trusted partners and personally inspected by a member of our family to ensure it meets the uncompromising standards of our house.

  • Cut: A diamond's brilliance begins with its cut. We choose stones that are precisely faceted to maximise beauty, sparkle, and proportion — never compromising on visual impact.
  • Carat: From delicate accents to important solitaires, we work with a wide range of carat weights, always prioritising balance and presence.
  • Colour: Our diamonds are graded across the spectrum, most often within the F-H range — where warmth and brilliance coexist beautifully. Exceptional old mine and larger solitaires are acquired on a case-by-case basis, with respect for their rarity and charm.
  • Clarity: We work with stones that meet our internal criteria for beauty and suitability. Whether flawless or naturally included, each diamond is chosen for how it contributes to the finished jewel.
EMERALDS

We work exclusively with natural emeralds from Zambia and Colombia — two of the world's most esteemed sources. Every emerald must meet our in-house standards for vivid colour, natural character, and structural integrity. Only stones with outstanding depth of green and minimum visible inclusions are accepted.

RUBIES

We source natural rubies only from Burma (Myanmar), renowned for their unmatched richness of red. Each ruby is carefully evaluated for purity of colour, natural origin, and brilliance, with only the finest passing our rigorous standards for clarity and saturation.

PEARLS

Our pearl selection includes both natural and cultured South Sea pearls, prized for their radiant lustre and generous size. Sourced from ethical marine farms, these pearls are assessed individually for surface quality, symmetry, and glow.
